AdvancedServo 8-Motor

Product Features
Control up to 8 RC servo motors or actuators with this controller board. Connects to your computer via USB.
Product Description
The AdvancedServo 8-Motor allows you to control the position, velocity, and acceleration of up to 8 RC servo motors. It requires a 6-15VDC external power supply; its switching power supply allows the controller to efficiently operate from 6 to 15 VDC and can be used with a wide range of batteries. All our power supplies can be used with the controller. The controller has a high resolution of 125 steps per degree; it measures the power consumption of each servo and its switching regulator protects the motors from overvoltage. It powers servo motors of up to 3.4 Amps. The controller continuously measures the current consumed by each motor with an accuracy of ±10%. The AdvancedServo connects directly to a computer’s USB port.

AdvancedServo 1-Motor

Product Features
Control one RC servo motor or actuator with this controller board. Powered solely by your computer via USB.
Product Description
The AdvancedServo 1-Motor allows you to control the position, velocity, and acceleration of one RC servo motor. The 1066 is powered solely by the USB cable – no additional power source is required. The controller has a high resolution of 125 steps per degree; it measures the power consumption of the servo, and powers servo motors of up to 450mA. The AdvancedServo connects directly to a computer’s USB port.
