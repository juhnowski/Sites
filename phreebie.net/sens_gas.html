Pressure Sensor

Product Features
This absolute gas pressure sensor plugs into an analog input and measures pressure from 20 kPa to 250 kPa.
Product Description
The 1115 measures absolute gas pressure from 20 to 250 kPa (2.9 to 36.3 psi) with a maximum error of ±1.5%. It is suitable for measuring vacuum, or atmospheric pressure; it can also be used as a crude barometer.

Differential Gas Pressure Sensor ± 25kPa

Product Features
This differential gas pressure sensor plugs into an analog input and measures pressure from -25 kPa to 25 kPa.
Product Description
The Differential Pressure Sensor is a dual ported gas pressure sensor that measures the pressure difference between the two ports. It can measure both positive and negative pressure from –25kPa to +25 kPa.
