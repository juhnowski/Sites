Magnetic Contact Switch

Product Features
This magnetic switch can detect magnets within 12mm and connects to a digital input. Magnet included.
Product Description
This magnetic contact switch is nice and small and is designed for flush or countersink installations. The switch handles gaps of up to 3/4 inch between the magnet and the switch. The switch will turn the digital input "on" when the magnet is within 3/4" (19 mm) of the switch, and will turn it "off" when the contact is broken by moving the magnet more than 1" away.

Roller Micro Switch SPDT (Bag of 2)

Product Features
Micro switch with a roller on the lever. Good for limit switches and more.
Product Description
